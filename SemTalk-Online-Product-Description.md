# SemTalk® Online Product Description

**Semtation GmbH** develops and distributes SemTalk® Online, a tool for business process modeling and ontology editing. For more than 20 years Semtation GmbH has supported customers in documenting workflows, identifying and providing metadata for documents and other content related projects.

**Semtation GmbH** provides **SemTalk® Online** along with basic customer services. If required, **Semtation GmbH** offers additional customer support products such as support for AI-based document indexing or process management to insure a more effective and optimized workplace.

Our goal is to enable our customers to manage their corporate knowledge and to use process management as a sustainable tool for optimally managing and conitually improving their processes.
